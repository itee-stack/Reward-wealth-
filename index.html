Company Heritage

Reward Investments and Services Ltd is a private limited liability company incorporated in 1995 with the sole aim of operating in the capital market. We undertake diverse business activities including stockbroking, investment advisory, portfolio management, and consultancy services.

The company was formed out of the sincere need to provide efficient capital market services to all our clients—individuals, corporate, and institutional investors alike. With the expected increase in capital market activities now and in the near future, we are positioned professionally and resource-wise to provide a wide range of investment services to meet client expectations.

Expert Investment Tips

Diversify Your Portfolio

Spread investments across different sectors and asset classes to reduce risk. Aim for a balanced mix of growth and defensive stocks aligned with your risk tolerance.

Start Early & Invest Regularly

Time in market beats timing the market. Regular investments compound significantly over time. Use our calculator to plan your investment journey systematically.

Monitor & Rebalance Quarterly

Review your portfolio regularly. Rebalance when allocations drift significantly from targets to maintain your desired risk level and investment objectives.

Understand Before You Invest

Never invest in something you don't understand. Research company fundamentals, industry trends, and macroeconomic factors before committing capital.
